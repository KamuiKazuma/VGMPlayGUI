VGMPlayGUI - Installation Instructions:
    
    1.      Install Valley Bell's VGMPlay for Windows. This is
        available at: (http://www.smspower.org/Music/InVgm).
        
    2.      Launch VGMPlayGUI, and head to the 'Options' menu.
    
    3.      Input or browse for your VGMPlay.exe file's path.
    
    4.      Click 'OK'.
    
    5.      Enjoy the magic of VGM.
